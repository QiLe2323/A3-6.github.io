User Requirements

These functional requirements define the essential interactions needed to make the museum experience playful, immersive, and collaborative.

Requirement #1

Role-playing Interaction

Functional || Event/Use Case #: TBA

Description: The product shall allow users to assume a historical character role to experience the museum from that perspective.
Rationale: Transforming passive observation into active participation is a core goal. Role-playing allows users to engage deeply with exhibits and immerse themselves in museum narratives.
Source: Stakeholders / User Research
Fit Criteria: Users can select a character and interact with at least one exhibit through that perspective. The system tracks the selected role and displays corresponding narrative content.
Customer Satisfaction5
Customer Dissatisfaction2
DependenciesNone
ConflictNone
Supporting MaterialsProject design document, exhibition script
HistoryRaised by Project Team, April 18th, 2026
Requirement #2

AR-based Exploration

Functional || Event/Use Case #: TBA

Description: The product shall support AR-based interactions with real museum exhibits, including scanning artifacts to unlock story fragments and triggering virtual characters or scenes.
Rationale: Bridging physical objects and digital narratives enhances visitor engagement and immersion.
Source: Stakeholders / User Research
Fit Criteria: Scanning at least one exhibit triggers a virtual animation or story fragment. AR interactions must function across multiple exhibits in the museum.
Customer Satisfaction5
Customer Dissatisfaction3
DependenciesAR-capable devices
ConflictNone
Supporting MaterialsAR design specification, 3D asset library

User Stories

Main Requirement
As a visitor, I want the museum interactive system to be more like a story I can step into, so that I can explore the exhibits actively and really feel like I’m part of the museum.
Sub-Requirements
As a visitor, I want to pick a historical character to play, so that I can see the exhibits from their perspective.
As a visitor, I want to scan exhibits with AR, so that I can unlock parts of the story and meet virtual characters.
As a visitor, I want the system to have tasks and levels, so that I can complete challenges, unlock new parts of the story, and track my progress.
As a visitor, I want to interact with other visitors, so that I can share experiences, collaborate, and make the museum visit more fun together.
As a visitor, I want the system to give me instant feedback, so that I can immediately see what happens after I take an action and stay engaged.
