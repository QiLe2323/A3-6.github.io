Our Team

Our team is a multidisciplinary group of four members collaborating on the project “My Stories in Museums.” We aim to design an immersive AR-based role-playing experience that transforms traditional museum visits into interactive storytelling journeys.

By integrating technologies such as AR interaction, QR/NFC scanning, and real-time user collaboration, our system allows visitors to step into the roles of historical figures. Users can unlock narrative fragments by scanning exhibits, interact with other players, and collectively advance through the storyline.

Each team member contributes specialized skills across research, design, development, and evaluation, ensuring both the creative depth and technical feasibility of the project.

Personas

These personas were generated with the help of AI to represent typical users of our museum interaction project. They help our team better understand visitor needs, improve interaction design, and make the museum experience more engaging and accessible.

◍ Emily Zhang

Emily Zhang

Primary Persona

Age: 21

Role: University student and frequent museum visitor

Background: Emily often visits museums, galleries, and cultural exhibitions with friends. She is comfortable using mobile apps, scanning QR codes, and trying digital interactive features during a visit. She enjoys learning through visual and immersive experiences rather than reading long blocks of text.

Characteristics:

Confident with smartphones and digital tools
Curious and willing to explore interactive exhibits
Prefers engaging and visually rich experiences
Likes sharing interesting experiences on social media

Goals:

Understand exhibits in a more interesting way
Enjoy a personalised and interactive museum visit
Access information quickly without reading too much text

Pain Points:

Traditional exhibit labels can feel too long or boring
Some museum information is difficult to understand quickly
Interactive features are sometimes hidden or unclear

Needs: A museum interaction system that is intuitive, visually appealing, and easy to use on mobile devices, with clear guidance and engaging content.

◍ Mr. David Lee

Mr. David Lee

Secondary Persona

Age: 52

Role: Parent and occasional museum visitor

Background: David visits museums with his family a few times a year. He is interested in history and culture, but he is not very confident with new digital systems. He appreciates clear explanations and simple interactions that help him and his family enjoy the museum without confusion.

Characteristics:

Basic digital skills
Values clarity, simplicity, and practicality
Prefers step-by-step guidance
More interested in meaningful learning than complex technology

Goals:

Understand exhibition content easily
Use interactive functions without frustration
Have a pleasant and educational museum experience with family

Pain Points:

Complicated interfaces make him hesitant to interact
Too many buttons or options feel overwhelming
Lack of clear instructions reduces confidence in using digital features

Needs: A museum interaction system with simple navigation, large clear text, obvious instructions, and accessible content for visitors with lower confidence in technology.
